The Dress Code is Garden Party Attire. Women should wear flowy, floral, frilly, or fun colored maxi or midi dresses. Men should opt for khakis or neutral slacks, paired with a button down or linen shirt. If you need some ideas or have any questions please reach out to us!

Things To Do

Good Eats

Breakfast

Briarpatch

Taiy's Favorite:

My favorite dish at Briarpatch is their nutella french toast with strawberries! It is pain-perdu european style brioche and it’s the best french toast I’ve ever had!

Purple Ocean

Conner's Favorite:

Purple Ocean has the best acai bowls in town. If you want something fresh this is the place to go. I enjoy munching down on a Jobos bowl with peanut butter and you will too!

The Salty Donut

Taiy's Favorite:

The Salty has so many fun flavors of cake and brioche style donuts! My go-to is the cookies and cream donut holes or the brown butter and salt! Both of them are cake donuts and they are so good!

Another Broken Egg

Conner's Favorite:

Another Broken Egg has the best brunch variety with options for everyone. I always look and try to find something new on their seasonal menus, but the chicken and waffles are always a hit!

Lunch

Crepevine

Taiy's Favorite:

Crepevine is a must do for pretty much all of our friends when they are in town! I always build my own crepe with chicken, swiss, cajun and ranch sauce, tomato, and spinach! The cajun and ranch is amazing! If you are feeling dessert, definitely try the sweet tooth!

Mediterranean Street Food

Conner's Favorite:

Their gyros hit different. This is the most authentic Mediterranean food in town. It is a tiny drive up spot, but their food definitely comes from the soul. I always get their traditional gyro with tzatziki.

Hawkers

Taiy's Favorite:

If you’re in the mood for Asian food, Hawkers has a great variety. I love their pad thai that comes with chicken and shrimp! Their dishes are fun to share at the table and try a bit of everything.

4 Rivers

Conner's Favorite:

4 Rivers is a local favorite with tasty bbq and even tastier sides. Their burnt ends melt is my absolute favorite. It is not a trip to 4 Rivers if you don’t leave with their crispy cream bread pudding.

Dinner

Sushi Sake

Taiy's Favorite:

Sushi Sake is for sure my favorite sushi spot in town! The Miami Heat roll is my go-to, but all of their rolls are great! I also love their fried shrimp shumai appetizer! If you want some sake, the strawberry one is so good.

Agave Azul

Conner's Favorite:

Agave Azul is a tex-mex restaurant with multiple locations around Orlando. It can be a little crowded at times but it is alway a good time. They have some huge margaritas and some delicious elote. My favorite though would have to be the blackened shrimp burrito!

Pannullo's

Taiy's Favorite:

The perfect Italian spot right on Park Ave! Conner and I love hitting up Pannullo’s for their happy hour pasta. Their lobster ravioli is amazing and always a great choice!

Maki Hibachi

Conner's Favorite:

Maki Hibachi is a great place for good vibes and good food. While they don’t cook in front of you, the restaurant is very cute and instagram-able. My favorite dish is the steak and calamari hibachi dinner, but everything I have tried there was good so you can’t go wrong!

Sweets & Snacks

Gideon's Bakehouse

Taiy's Favorite:

The best cookies in Orlando without a doubt! Gideon’s is an award winning bakehouse that has half pound cookies! My favorite is the pistachio toffee or the banana bread, be sure to get there earlier in the day to make sure they don’t sell out!

Jeremiahs

Conner's Favorite:

Jeremiah’s Italian Ice is the place to go to soothe your sweet tooth. With multiple locations and rotating flavors everyone is sure to find something they can enjoy! I love their cookies and creme italian ice layered with vanilla custard in a gelati.

Choulala

Taiy's Favorite:

Another great little spot on Park Ave! Choulala makes fine pastries of all kinds, but they are known for their choux puffs. Their flavors change seasonally and they always have something for everyone!

Krave Tea

Conner's Favorite:

Krave tea is the perfect place to get a refreshing sip. With many different flavor options and very instagrammable drinks you are bound to find something you like. My personal favorite is the taro milk tea with black sugar boba.
